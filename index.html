DMatrix: Toward fast and accurate queries in graph stream

On this website we present additional information about our paper "DMatrix: Toward fast and accurate queries in graph stream" published in "Computer Networks".

Traffic measurement tool

This tool is built on DMatrix for application traffic statistics, queries, and analysis.

Top applications and top sources

The traffic measurement tool can get the top applications and top sources in specific epochs and the corresponding traffic volume.

Top applications of top sources

The traffic measurement tool can analyze the top applications of the top sources, showing the percentage of heavy applications. The table on the right analyzes the destination of the HTTPS/HTTP traffic that accounts for a large proportion.
